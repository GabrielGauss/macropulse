What MacroPulse Delivers

📊

4-State Regime Classification

Expansion, Tightening, Risk-Off, Recovery — each day the HMM produces probability distributions across all four regimes.

💧

Net Liquidity Proxy

Fed balance sheet minus TGA minus Reverse Repo. The single most important variable for risk-asset direction, computed daily.

🧠

PCA Latent Factors

Dimensionality reduction across macro and market features into 3-4 interpretable latent factors capturing ~80% of variance.

⚡

Real-Time WebSocket

Stream regime updates the instant the daily pipeline completes. Build dashboards and trading systems that react in real time.

🔔

Regime Change Alerts

Email and webhook notifications when the macro regime transitions. Never miss a macro inflection point.

📈

Model Drift Monitoring

Continuous monitoring of PCA variance stability, regime persistence, and feature distribution shift with automated warnings.
